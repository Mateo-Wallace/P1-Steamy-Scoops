Steamy Scoops

Hello World! We Are The Bu10 Bois™!

Our Mission

Our goal is to bring the wonders of buttons and all manners of pronunciations of the word button to the world. This web application was created with the guise of nerdy video game news in order to trick you into seeing more buttons! Why you ask? To spread the gospel of our Primordial Deity John of course!

How We Met

The Bu10 Bois™ met during September 2022 at the UCF Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. A group of like-minded nerds who previously were already in a study group, they banded together during their first group project in order to create this! The wonderful Steamy Scoops application you see before you.
